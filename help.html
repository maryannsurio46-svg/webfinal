Help & Documentation

Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard provides a monthly snapshot: total income, total expenses, and net savings. It also shows a quick view of your budget progress and financial goals.

How Budgeting Works


Set a monthly limit for any expense category. Log transactions in the 'Budgeting' tab. Progress bars turn yellow at 80% and red when exceeded.


Data Persistence

This application uses *Firebase Firestore* for real-time, persistent storage. Your data is stored securely under your unique user ID and will be available every time you open the prototype.


Instructions


1. Navigate to 'Budgeting' to set your monthly spending limits and log transactions (both income and expenses).

2. Use the 'Reports' tab to view your complete transaction history and delete any entries.

3. Monitor your progress against your budgets and savings goals on the 'Dashboard'.
