About Money Tracker

Our Story

Money Tracker was born in a college dorm room when we realized how difficult it was to manage finances as a student. Between tuition, books, rent, food, and occasional social activities, money seemed to disappear faster than lecture notes before finals. 

We noticed that existing budgeting tools weren't designed with student life in mind. They were too complex, didn't account for irregular income from part-time jobs or occasional parental support, and didn't speak our language. That's why we created Money Tracker – by students, for students.

Our Mission

To empower students with simple, intuitive tools to take control of their finances, reduce money-related stress, and build healthy financial habits that last beyond graduation.

What Makes Us Different

Built for Student Life · Irregular Income Tracking: Whether it's a part-time job, side hustle, or allowance from home· Semester-Based Budgeting: Aligns with your academic calendar, not just monthly cycles · Student-Specific Categories: Textbooks, meal plans, campus events, and student discounts · Shareable Budgets: For roommates splitting rent and utilitie

Privacy & Security

We understand the sensitivity of financial data. Your information is encrypted and never sold. We don't require banking logins – we believe in transparency without compromising security.

Free Forever for Students

We're committed to keeping our core features completely free for students. No hidden fees, no premium walls for essential tools.

Our Values

Financial Education Over Restriction  We're not just about tracking expenses – we're about understanding them. Our resources help you learn about saving, investing, and smart spending. 

Community Driven

Features are inspired by student feedback. Our roadmap is shaped by what you tell us you need. Realistic, Not Restrictive We know student life includes both ramen nights and occasional treats. Our goal is balance, not deprivation.
