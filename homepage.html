About Us

Our Story
Money Tracker was born in a college dorm when Patricia and Mary Ann realised how difficult it was to manage finances as a student. Between transportation, books, rent, food, and occasional social activities, money seemed to disappear faster than lecture notes before finals.

We noticed that existing budgeting tools weren't designed with student life in mind. They were too complex, didn't account for irregular income from part-time jobs or occasional parental support, and didn't speak our language. That's why we created Money Tracker-by students, for students.

Our Mission
To empower students with simple, intuitive tools to take control of our finances, reduce money-related stress, and build healthy financial habits that last beyond graduation.
